## Overview

The Leap Employee Card displays an employee's avatar, name, role, department,
and leave balance in a compact card layout. It is designed for manager
dashboards and team views where a quick summary of each team member is needed.

### When to use

- In a manager dashboard to show a grid of direct reports
- On a team calendar page to identify employees alongside their balances
- In a search results list when looking up employees

### When not to use

- For detailed employee profiles with editable fields — use a dedicated profile
  page
- As a navigation element — use a standard link or button
- When displaying a single employee's full leave history — use Leap Timeline

## Formatting

### Anatomy

The Leap Employee Card consists of:

1. **Avatar** — circular image or initials fallback
2. **Employee name** — primary identifying text
3. **Role** — the employee's job title
4. **Department** (optional) — the employee's department
5. **Leave balance** (optional) — remaining leave days shown as a small badge

### Placement

Employee cards are typically arranged in a responsive grid. They work well in
dashboard panels, team overview pages, or as popover content on hover.
