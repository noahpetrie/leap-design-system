## What the component does

The Leap Balance Card communicates a leave balance using both a progress bar and
numeric text. This dual representation ensures the information is accessible to
users who cannot perceive the visual bar.

## Design recommendations

- Do not rely solely on colour to indicate a low balance state; the numeric text
  must also be present
- Ensure the progress bar fill colour meets a minimum 3:1 contrast ratio against
  the track background
- Keep the leave type label concise and descriptive

## Development considerations

- Use `role="progressbar"` with `aria-valuenow`, `aria-valuemin`, and
  `aria-valuemax` on the progress element
- Provide an `aria-label` such as "Vacation: 8 of 20 days used" so screen
  readers can convey the full context
- Ensure the component is included in the natural tab order when part of an
  interactive dashboard
