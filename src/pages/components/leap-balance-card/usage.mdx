## Overview

The Leap Balance Card component displays a single leave-type balance as a
compact card with a progress bar. It communicates at a glance how many days or
hours have been used out of a total allocation.

### When to use

- On a dashboard to show each leave type's remaining balance
- In a sidebar or widget area alongside a leave request form
- To summarise balances on an employee profile page

### When not to use

- To display a detailed breakdown of individual leave transactions — use Leap
  Timeline instead
- As a status indicator for a specific leave request — use Leap Status Badge
- When you need to show balances for many employees at once — use Leap Data
  Table

## Formatting

### Anatomy

The Leap Balance Card consists of:

1. **Leave type label** — the name of the leave category
2. **Progress bar** — visual representation of used vs. total
3. **Balance text** — numeric display of used and total values
4. **Unit indicator** — whether the balance is measured in days or hours

### Placement

Balance cards are typically arranged in a horizontal row or grid on a dashboard.
They work well inside summary panels, profile sidebars, or at the top of a leave
management page.
